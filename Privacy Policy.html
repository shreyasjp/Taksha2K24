Privacy Policy for Taksha'24 Website

Effective Date: 20 January 2024

Welcome to Taksha'24 Website, the official website for Taksha'24, a technological festival organized by The Department of Computer Science, St. Paul's College, Kalamassery. This Privacy Policy applies to the Taksha'24 website located at https://taksha2k24.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, utilize, and safeguard personal information obtained from users of our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information from participants:

  • Name: To personalize communication and address participants appropriately.
  • College Name: To understand the academic affiliations of participants and tailor event-related information.
  • Contact Number: To communicate important updates and information regarding the event.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, such as IP addresses, browser types, and device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Event Communication: To provide information and updates about the tech fest.
  • Personalization: To tailor our communications and content based on participants' preferences.
  • Analytics: To analyze website usage and improve our services.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer personal information to outside parties. However, we may share information with trusted third parties, including sponsors, who assist us in conducting our tech fest or servicing participants, provided that they agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect the personal information we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Consent

By providing us with your personal information, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy and the collection, use, and disclosure of such information in accordance with this policy.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on the website.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at cs@stpauls.ac.in.

By using our website and participating in our tech fest, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Thank you for being part of Taksha'24!
