Description

Unleash your creative prowess in Creatrix, our web design contest. Elevate the visual aesthetics of the web and showcase your ability to weave digital wonders.

Event Structure

  • Participants are entrusted with the task of replicating the provided design with utmost accuracy.
  • External libraries and frameworks may not join the elegance; only the pure essence of vanilla HTML and CSS is permitted.
  • Originality is the crown jewel – any sign of plagiarism or code copying will dim the sparkle of your creation, leading to disqualification.
  • Teams, in their pursuit of elegance, are limited to a harmonious maximum of two members.
  • Participants will be assessed on their capacity to integrate design elements faithfully, replicate interactive features meticulously, ensure a user-friendly interface with responsive design, and demonstrate code finesse through clean, well-structured HTML and organized CSS.

Disclaimer

In consideration of scheduling constraints, participants engaging in Creatrix are kindly advised against simultaneous participation in BugBounty, Lethal Legion, Twist 'N' Turn, and ThinkQuake. Your understanding is valued, and we extend encouragement for an immersive and enriching experience exclusively within the realm of Creatrix.
