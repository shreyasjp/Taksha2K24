Description

Engage in the intellectual symphony of Twist 'n' Turn. Solve the Rubik's Cube and showcase your algorithmic finesse in this mind-bending competition.

Event Structure

  • Engage in the art of cubing where every competitor faces the same thrilling challenge with official and uniform scrambles.
  • Hone your skills with a dedicated 15 seconds of inspection time before the enchanting Twist'N'Turn journey commences.
  • The solve commences with a touch of hands on the timer and gracefully concludes upon their release, encapsulating the elegance of speed cubing.
  • With a maximum time for cube inspection and a distinct window for the solve itself, competitors showcase their finesse with a touch of urgency.
