Description

ThinkQuake is the stage for ideas that resonate with brilliance. Pitch your innovative concepts and witness the seismic impact of your thoughts.

Event Structure

  • Any innovative idea related to computer science can be presented.
  • Presentation groups must consist of a minimum of two members and a maximum of three members.
  • Total presentation time is set at 20 minutes, encompassing the introduction, main presentation, and conclusion.
  • Following the presentation, a 10-minute Q&A session provides an opportunity for the audience and judges to engage with the presenting group.
  • Groups are encouraged to enhance their presentations with visual aids such as slides, charts, or diagrams.
  • Final judgment is based on the clarity of the idea, presentation skills, depth of content, creativity, and the ability to address questions effectively during the Q&A.

Disclaimer

In the spirit of fostering diverse engagements, participants in the ThinkQuake event are kindly advised against concurrent participation in Creatrix, BugBounty, Lethal Legion, and Twist 'N' Turn. We appreciate your cooperation and encourage you to focus exclusively on the ThinkQuake experience.
